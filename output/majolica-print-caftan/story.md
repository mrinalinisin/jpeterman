# Majolica Print Caftan

Hydra, Greece.

Square-stone buildings from quay to hilltop. Aegean blue water.

She meets me at the boat, and we linger over ouzas at O Peiratis. Talk of art and politics for hours. Catalogue our favorite Prine songs.

Toast to Leonard and Marianne.

“How does an artist find a muse?” I wonder aloud.

Purely rhetorical, of course.
