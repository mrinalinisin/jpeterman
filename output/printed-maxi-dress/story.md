# English Garden Caftan

Insouciance.

The thing about a dress like this is how it makes you feel like your most glorious days are still ahead. 

Starting with this one.

You could spend the month of September by Lake Louise trying to get the alpine blue just right on canvas. 

It could be just you and a typewriter in a cabin outside of Taos.

Maybe finally learn to play the guitar. 

You with him on the Via Chiantigiana winding through Chianti country. 

Live music at Woodstock (now with more creature comforts). 

Your thoughts coalesce. A page turns.

What if? becomes the question you can’t shake. 

A lovely insouciance takes over. A plan emerges.  

English Garden Caftan (No. 7036). Charming V-neck floral caftan with the soul of a maxi dress. Flowing, floral, fabulously free. Shell and lining are 100% viscose. Camisole slip. Self tie at bust. Gathered under bust and at shoulder on neckline. Slip adjuster. Imported.
