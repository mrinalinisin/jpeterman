# Cropped Wide Leg Jean

Old South Wharf, Nantucket.

You'll need a proper shop bell above the door.

Sturdy shelves. Old hardwood floors.

A shop dog (essential).

A loft with a view of the water. (This is important.)

A studio out back with a pottery wheel and a kiln. More shelves.

Aunt Meredith's waxed canvas apron and a hook by the door for hanging it.

You could pretend it's just for a year, but the boxes of books say otherwise.

All of this is to say, your new sense of freedom will need a place to feel at home.

Cropped Wide Leg Jean (No. 6448). A shortened take on the classic denim wide leg. Of then, of now. The high-waisted fit is infinitely flattering to all figures. Cotton/spandex blend for comfort and stretch. Two patch pockets in front, two in back. The belt is long enough to tie twice, with enough left over to fall in dramatic fashion. (Of course, what is knotted can be undone, with the right motivation.) Antique brass zipper and one antique brass shank button. Detachable self-wrap belt. Double needle stitching. Contrast antique brass-tone stitching. Imported.
