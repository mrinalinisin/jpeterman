# Your Weekend Dress

Weekends.

There isn’t the pressure. The signature lines. The panicked eyeballs looking your way for the life preserver. There are no power suits or call sheets. No to-do lists. No assistants.

There is, however, the cabin in the Adirondacks. The one your great-grandfather gifted your grandfather. Gave to you over the boys in the family because you get it.

Twenty-minute drive for a cell phone signal. The swaths of jasmine and lavender. The wild turkeys. The way the sun dapples through the big birch. The way your dress flutters on the porch swing.

Yeah, you get it.

Your Weekend Dress (No. 5392). 100% silk sheer crinkle georgette in one of our most gorgeous prints. Short flutter sleeves at princess seams. Pullover-style fit-and-flare, mid-calf length, French seam finishes. Black drawcord with tassels at waist. Fully lined in coral crepe. Made in India.
