# Annata's Floral Caftan

Vibrant.

Without hesitating, Annata can tell you the 15 best books she read last year, the definitive way to grill a steak, and the one person she would save on that hypothetical sinking ship.

Her favorite word is vibrant.

Her least favorite word: almost.

The last three times I saw her, she was wearing this caftan, looking more beautiful, confident, and expressive with each encounter.

They say youth is wasted on the young.

Don’t believe it.
