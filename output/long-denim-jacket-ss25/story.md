# Long Denim Jacket

Long Denim Jacket (No. J5970). Stone-and-enzyme-washed pure denim. Antique brass shank buttons down the front and on the sleeve cuffs, the chest pockets, and the epaulettes. Loving and shapely fitted style that flares out to a subtle A-line at the sweep. Patch pockets with flap at the chest and angled-welt pockets at hips. Slightly curved hem. Imported.
