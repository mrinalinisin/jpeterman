# The Carnevale Duster

The Carnevale Duster (No. J8860). Embroidered button-up duster that transforms into a dress with several buttons done up nicely (the exact number is entirely up to you and your whims). Comfortable, midi length cotton shell embellished with rather glorious embroidery. Velvet center front placket and velvet panel at center back. Side slits add interest to the proceedings. Two side pockets. Velvet panels made from a poly/spandex blend. Perfect for the festivities with a mask of your choice. Imported.
