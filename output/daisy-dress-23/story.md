# The Marguerite Dress

The Marguerite Dress (No. 7509). The story here begins and ends with marguerites (daisies). Outer and inner layers drape you deliciously in a silky viscose/cotton blend. Full, pleated skirt falls below knee. Sweetheart neckline at front, scoop neckline at back. Front and back princess seams and bust darts. Cap sleeves. Three covered shank buttons at front bodice. Invisible zipper at wearer’s left. Imported.
