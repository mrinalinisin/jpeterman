# Electric Light Dress

Electric Light Dress (No. 5815). Gold stretch metallic knit bodice (70% nylon, 30% metallic) with boat neckline. Knee length straight skirt in a black and gold poly and metallic jacquard pattern. Figure-flattering black velvet ribbon at waist provides a wonderful line of contrast between the two striking patterns. Vent at center back. Bodice is lined in knit tricot; skirt is lined in taffeta. Imported.
