# Florentina Ikat Maxi Skirt

Florentina Ikat Maxi Skirt (No. J8138). The story begins with a flattering waist yoke that falls into three tiered layers of breezy cotton to a terrifically fetching conclusion just at the ankle. Lined in cool, airy cotton. Invisible zipper and hook and eye at wearer’s left side seam. Destined to become your most interesting travel skirt, even if you’re only venturing to the local la panetteria. Imported.
