# Himalayan Handknit Hearth Stockings

HIMALAYAN HANDKNIT HEARTH STOCKINGS

Our genuine Himalayan stockings pay proper tribute to the art and artisans of the Himalayas. Kaligarh, which means ‘artisan’ in Nepal, continue the traditions of their ancestors by crafting these exquisite holiday stockings in the vibrant colors and unique designs of the Himalayan mountain range. Individually hand-dyed and hand-knit using thick, 100% wool. Made in Nepal.
