# Paradox Readers

Paradox Readers (No. J8024). Confident, slightly bold readers in a floral blue pattern. Adjustable nose pads. They are made with dual acetate lamination.

Four magnifications:

1.5 (just enough),

2.0 (snaps printed word back into sharp focus),

2.5 (a little stronger),

3.0 (if your partner is reading this for you).

Carrying case included.

Frame weight is average 136mm

Material: Acetate

Shape: Geometric and Hexagonal
