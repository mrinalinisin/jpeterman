# The New Swan Blouse

Lillian’s Annual Spring Fête.

It should always be this easy to spot the hostess.

Lillian always wears exactly the right thing to her own party. Manages to look both relaxed (she is) and just off the plane from Capri (only just).

There’s talk that she’s related to Slim Keith on her mother’s side.

That Warhol painted her twice.

That there’s a deed in her name to a private island off the coast of… somewhere fabulous, let’s say.

I happen to know all of this is true.

But you’ll never hear it from her.
