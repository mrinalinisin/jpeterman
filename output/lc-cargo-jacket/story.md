# Cargo Jacket

Urban Assault.
In Barbados and Aruba you’ll be arrested for wearing camouflage.

In Austin or Prague you will not.

However, you will probably not go unnoticed in this cargo jacket so if invisibility is your intention I suggest a ghillie suit and a jungle. If chic urban assault is the Major’s orders…

Carry on.

The Cargo Jacket (No. 4613). 97% cotton with a little stretchy stuff. 4 button hidden placket with 1 angled button at placket’s top. Storm strap at underside of collar. Point collar with decorative stitching (5 rows of topstitching and large zigzag stitch at underside of collar). Self fabric half moon and locker loop at inside of center back. Shoulder epaulettes with button. Adjustable drawstring at inside waist tunnel. Four patch pockets at front with flap and hidden button closure. Adjustable button closure at sleeve tab. Imported.
