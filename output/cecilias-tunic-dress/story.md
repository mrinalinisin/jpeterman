# Cecilia's Tunic Dress

Cecilia's Tunic Dress

Late April, just shy of May. Late morning, just shy of noon. Cecilia, in contrast, is just shy of exactly nothing. Fully stunning. Vibrant, clever. Entirely prepossessing in this stunning shade of sage. We're poring over upholstery fabrics and she asks if I remember the name of a certain rug merchant in the medina in Essaouira. I confess, I do not. But I'm not likely to forget her dress. Cecilia's Tunic Dress (No. 7516). Richly-hued floral print tunic with the personality of a shirtdress. Deliciously-cool 100% cotton with an intriguing allover block print. Wide neck band and placket. Shirring at neck in front and back. Flattering bust darts. Narrow self-tie belt at waist. Imported.
