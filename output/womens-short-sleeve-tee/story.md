# Women's Short-Sleeve Tee

To a Tee.

Every now and then, I go out and do a little research in the field.

As Thornton Wilder put it, “There’s nothing like eavesdropping to show you that the world outside your head is different from the world inside your head.”

I’m always pleased with the discovery. The conversation, surrounding the not-sosimple T-shirt, in various high-to-higher-end stores to ridiculous-end stores, went like this:

“My Yorkie couldn’t fit into this.”

“Great cut. You’d think they’d make it in short sleeves.”

“If this were just a tad longer, I’d love it.”

“Hmm. Now this is perfect.”
