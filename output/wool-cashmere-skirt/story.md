# Wool & Cashmere Skirt

Imported from Camelot.

There was a time, a brief time, in the early 1960s, when we all believed anything was possible. Including elegance.

The gracious, charming, self-assured kind of elegance, that is, not the stuck-up kind…Pablo Casals and Robert Frost stopping by the White House for the evening to entertain us.

I reached back to that moment (real, not legendary) for this gracious knit sweater top and skirt. 

They’re made of a luxurious blend of wool and cashmere. It stretches but keeps a smooth, fresh look throughout the day and into the evening. 

Perfect for the busy season (first Monday of September on) when your dance card fills up.

We could all certainly use some elegance again.

Wool and Cashmere Skirt (No. 7110). Luscious blend of 90% wool and 10% cashmere in a striking midi length knit skirt. Comfortable elastic waistband. Imported.
