# Priam Handmade Leather Passport Cover

Start an international adventure with our new Priam Handmade Leather Passport Cover. The cover is made using full-grain crazy horse leather. This distressed leather is beautiful and will develop an amazing patina over time.

The Priam Handmade Leather Passport Cover is a great way to protect your passport.

Please note: Due to natural variations between hides and dye lots, there may be slight differences in coloration and texture in the handcrafted leather passport covers. This item is natural vegetable tanned.
