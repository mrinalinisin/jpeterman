# Irish Heritage Cardigan

Irish Heritage Cardigan (No. J8664). 100% luxurious merino wool. Indulgent. The super-fine knit passes through 27 pairs of hands before exiting the factory. Long, ribbed sleeves. Relaxed edge-to-edge construction. We suggest ordering a size down if you want something more form-fitting. Hand wash. Do not tumble dry. Made in Ireland.
