# 1959 Party Dress

1959 Party Dress (No. J5938). Lustrous cotton poplin. Distinctive V-neckline with point collar extending to the shoulders. Waistline seam, four box pleats for volume and drape. Princess seams and back darts shape the bodice. Mid-calf skirt, on-seam side pockets, fully lined in acetate taffeta, invisible back zipper. Imported.
