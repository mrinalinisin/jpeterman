# Louise Brooks Coat

Louise Brooks Coat (No. J7734), like the one she wore when she crossed the Atlantic to be worshiped by G.W. Pabst’s camera. Soft, warm Italian wool-and-cashmere blend, satin lined. Single-button closure and 26” back vent for lots of movement. The shawl collar (10” wide max.) and cuffs are luxurious shearling. Imported.
