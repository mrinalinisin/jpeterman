# Turkish Kilim Loafers

Turkish Kilim Loafers (No. J7708). Handmade from richly-patterned Turkish kilim rugs. Wool kilim rug upper with a ¾” stacked leather sole and leather lining. As each design is unique, prepare to be surprised and delighted by the pattern you receive.

Women’s UK sizes.

Sizing: Please measure your foot ahead of ordering from big toe to heel. If you fall on the half-size usually, we first recommend ordering the two sizes you fall between and returning the pair that is not a fit. If you do not want to do this and fall on the half size, we recommend sizing down. 

CARE INSTRUCTIONS

Treat the sole with a leather protectant spray and clean with leather cleaner. For the rug upper, wipe with a dry microfiber cloth.

Made by hand in Istanbul, Turkey.
