# Women' s Picasso Mule

Women's Picasso Mules (No. J8897). Handmade with a 700-year-old Turkish shoe-making technique using five different kinds of leather. Woven together with cotton thread dipped in beeswax. Designed to mold to your foot, they start off snug and become perfectly yours the longer you wear them. Fit is true to size. Made in Turkey.

Women's European sizes: 37 (US 6) through 42 (US 11).
