# Forever Plaid Pant

Forever Plaid.

There will always be an England. 

There will always be plaid.

A comforting notion in the ‘40s for a nation.

These quintessentially British plaid leggings stirred both comforting feelings of home and perhaps some other feelings that were less homey. Still does.

Plaid Leggings (No. 6823). Slightly preppy, seriously stylish plaid legging. Cotton/poly blend with a just-right measure of stretchy stuff. Imported.
