# Ruffle Chiffon Dress

A Dangerous Woman.

I keep watching her.

We all do.

She elegantly brushes her cards back to the table.

In her previous life, she was Beatriz Galindo.

In this one, she doesn’t use titles (not even the ones she’s earned). Her essays are brilliant, even in translation.

Her osso bucco, the same.

Tonight, at this little casino in northern Spain (in the Palacio de los Condes), I remain a fascinated spectator as she gracefully flips over a straight flush, King high.

The men at the table smile.

Lovesick. Broke.
