# Lace Pants

Lavish Lace. 

A fanciful detail as charming as lace can never be a bad thing.

Don’t forget, entire countries have argued over its invention.

Was it the Italians? The Dutch? The Dutch do make lace fences which is quite a statement.

Laceophiles have been around since the 1600s. It’s been seen on men. Women. Wedding dresses. Battle armor (yes battle armor).

Lace is intricate. Pretty. Highly feminine. It makes you feel lavish. Special. It’s also angelic yet slightly devilish. It gives you a taste of what’s underneath but not the whole thing. And that mystery is always a good thing.

Maybe try it out on casual Friday and see what happens.

The Lace Pant (No. 6460). Mid-rise, cropped ankle-length trouser with a raw, cut-lace hem. Contrast fused backing (see above: mystery). Side pockets. Zip fly with hook and bar closure. Two back double-welt pockets. Belt loops. Imported.
