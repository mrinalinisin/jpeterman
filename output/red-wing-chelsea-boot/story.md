# Chelsea Boot

Go Anywhere. 

You took two moments to slide these on--look how easy that was. And now you an go anywhere. Hiking Overlook? Sure (after all, it's a Red Wing boot). Cocktails at Stockade? Yes. Dinner at Le Canard? Yep.

Wearing other shoes will take some convincing.

Chelsea Boot (No. 6393). Versatile, easy-wearing, supportive. Slip-on construction eased by elastic insets. Just formal enough, just rugged enough, and they fit better than most your other shoes. The "roughout" leather is crafted using a reverse-suede technique which makes it more durable and avoids splitting and thinning. Made in the USA by Red Wing.
