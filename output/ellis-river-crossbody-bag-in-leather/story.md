# Ellis River Crossbody Bag in Leather

Stitched in our workshop in Maine, named after one of our favorite waterways, the Ellis River Crossbody Bag is our smallest and most lightweight bag. Crafted entirely of genuine  leather. Guaranteed to delight.

Made in Maine
Available in Bison leather or Cowhide leather

Genuine top-grain leather bottom and straps

Slim width to hold phone, small purse or credit card case
Fully lined interior

Front zippered pocket (for keys or other easy to reach items)

Top closure zipper (to keep bag safe)

Adjustable leather strap (total length 52”) with swivel clasps; can be used as a crossbody or an over-the-shoulder purse
9-1/2”w x 9” h; weight 11 oz
