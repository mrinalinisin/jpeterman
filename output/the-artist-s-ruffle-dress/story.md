# The Artist's Ruffle Dress

To the Troublemakers.

“The dealings with artists, for instance, require great prudence; they are acquainted with all classes of society, and for that very reason dangerous...and when you have too much to do with them, you are sure to have des ennuis…” 

—King Leopold to Queen Victoria, 10th October 1845

Those artists, those troublemakers.

You, for instance, are possessed of more than your fair share of creativity.

Look what you accomplished with a palette of greys and browns all winter long.

Imagine what you can do with these dazzling colors.
