# Sensational Sequins Gown

Sensational Sequins.

 

She has strong feelings about tinsel (against), the Eiffel Tower light show (strongly opposed, on principle), and light pollution in general (“A real crisis, Peterman.”).

Her opinions on sequins, however, are entirely enthusiastic.

“Cover me, please,” she tells me. “Head to toe. Make me sparkle.”

I find I'm powerless to do anything but oblige.

 

Sensational Sequins Gown (No. J7906). A sequin-drenched stunner for your most momentous holiday soirées. Full-length sheath dress in sensational electric blue features a very va-va-voom V-neckline with a flattering twist just below the bust. Darts for shaping. Crepe de chine lining.
