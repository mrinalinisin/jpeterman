# Gloria Silk Dress

Be Good at Being Bad.

This dress brings it all back to me.

Her voice, like maple syrup (Amber grade) laced with bourbon. Foxy features. Quirky mouth. Eyes that frequently narrowed into appraising slits.

(“It wasn’t the way I looked at a man,” she said, “it was the thought behind it.”) 

Gloria Grahame was the best good bad girl (or was it the other way around?) in Hollywood. At my awkward age, I didn’t know exactly what was going on, but watching

her on the screen was strangely exciting.

Nobody I knew had a mother even remotely like that.

The Gloria Silk Dress (No. 6777) in 100% silk satin. Form-fitting. Smoldering. Fitted knee-length dress with ¾ set-in sleeves. Contrast button and black mesh applique at center front placket and sleeve hem. Self-covered buttons down center front. 

If your ex sees you in this one, it will put a dagger though his heart. Imported.
