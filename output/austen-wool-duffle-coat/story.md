# Long Lake Wool Duffle Coat

Long Lake Wool Duffle Coat (No. J8659). Classic duffle coat style in 910 gm “Airforce Blue” felted wool. Hooded classic duffle with large vertical-slit pockets and wooden toggles with jute rope fastening. Generous fit. Fixed throat tab keeps the weather outside where it belongs. A touch of polyamide increases durability and reduces pilling. Adjustable tabs at cuffs. Reinforced shoulder detail. Stylish, sturdy, warm and practical. Made in England.
