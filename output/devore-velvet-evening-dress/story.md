Devoré Velvet Evening Dress (No. 7383). We reached back in time exactly far enough to find the perfect inspiration for this holiday-ready dress. Exquisite 100% viscose burnout devoré velvet in an intricate allover pattern. The color, a deep chocolate brown. Nearly black but not quite. Flattering empire waist seam. Bias cut skirt. Mermaid sweep at hem. Fully lined. Invisible zipper at wearer’s left side seam. Imported.
