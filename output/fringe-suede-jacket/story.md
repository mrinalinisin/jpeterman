# Fringe Suede Jacket

Fringe Suede Jacket (No. J7047). Classic Western fringe jacket by way of Italy, where they know more than most about how to make such things just right. Artfully and meticulously constructed from 100% goat suede. Lined in Korean twill. Suede-covered buttons. Fringe along front and back yoke and sleeve seams. Notched collar. Back and front yokes come to a point. Slightly cropped. Made in India.
