# Miss Greene's Suede Jacket

Miss Greene’s Suede Jacket (No. 5944). I wouldn’t call this urban prairie chic, but some rather heady publications might. Luxurious goat suede fully lined in a white-with-rose pinstripe.

Slightly cropped, modern style. Wonderfully simple and nostalgic silver metal grommets with self-ties at the front neckline. Also captivating with simple denim skirts and floral dresses. Made in India.
